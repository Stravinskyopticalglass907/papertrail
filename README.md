## 📑 How to Use papertrail

### Reading PDFs Page by Page

- Enter the page numbers you want to focus on, such as `1`, or a range like `3-5`.

- papertrail uses AI to pull out key points from those pages.

- If the page has little text, papertrail can read images and charts using OCR (text recognition).

### Taking Notes

- Click the note button 📝 to open a yellow note area on the sidebar.

- Write your thoughts or important points while reading.

- The note is saved instantly when you leave the typing area.

### Managing Your Work

- The sidebar hides when you leave the PDF tab to keep your workspace clean.

- papertrail reloads your past notes instantly when you return to a document.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

- If the sidebar does not appear, ensure you opened a PDF in Chrome.

- If AI features do not work, check your internet connection.

- For any crashes, restart Chrome or your PC.

- If notes are missing, check that you stayed on the page long enough to save them.
